Current projects include:

* **[Gordian Envelope](https://developer.blockchaincommons.com/envelope/).** Our privacy-first storage system, including elision capabilities, continues to be the focus of many projects, most recently including [Garner](https://developer.blockchaincommons.com/garner/) and [Hubert](https://developer.blockchaincommons.com/hubert/).
* **[XIDs](https://developer.blockchaincommons.com/xid/). Another use of Gordian Envelope, XIDs offer the model for [true self-sovereign identity](https://www.blockchaincommons.com/musings/XIDs-True-SSI/).
* **[Learning Bitcoin from the Command Line](https://github.com/BlockchainCommons/Learning-Bitcoin-from-the-Command-Line/blob/master/README.md). Our classic course offering hands-on experience learning Bitcoin is being updated in 2026 thanks to a grant from HRF.
* **[FROST](https://developer.blockchaincommons.com/frost/).** We brought our three years of FROST to a capstone in 2025 with our [Learning FROST from the Command Line course](https://learningfrost.blockchaincommons.com/), which includes hands-on work with tools we built to support FROST signing of Bitcoin transactions.
